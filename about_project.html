RefugeeUT's Accomplishments


For the Spring 2022 Semester, as part of the Communication 2110 Course (Interpersonal and Small Group Communication), a small group of Weber State University were tasked with working on a project to address social inequality in Utah's community. We began working with Utah Refugee Connection to raise supplies and funds.

Our goals for this project were:

  • Learn about refugees and access their needs. We asked ourselves
    • How can we help refugees and fight the social injustice that has occurred?
    • How can we make sure thatthese injustices donâ€™t happen here within our communities?
  • Raise awareness by sharing our information on our social media accounts and with flyers at our local grocery store.
  • Serve our refugee community by showing them that we value them as members of our community.
  • Fundraise and collect donations to stock the storehouse which provides refugees with basic needs so they can rebuild.

We started with designing some flyers which we shared on social media. We then also set up a booth outside of the Lees stores to raise funds and collect materials for donation.

Overall, we raised $268 in donations and $350 worth of materials.
